About Me

Hello! My name is Debojyoti Chakraborty, and I am a passionate and dedicated professional with a diverse skill set in web development, graphic design, and automation. I am 30 years old and have built my career from the ground up, starting from a background that initially didn’t focus on a clear career path, but with determination and hard work, I found my true calling in the world of technology and design.

I completed my schooling at Kendriya Vidyalaya and my college education at Calcutta University. While I didn't initially take my career path too seriously, today I’m proud to say that I have found a rewarding profession that continuously excites and challenges me.

Currently, I am working at Siti Networks as a Researcher. My role here is multifaceted: I create 7-Days FPC (Fixed Point Charts) for TV channels, design festival logos for Tubers TV Channel, and work on graphic design tasks such as creating standees, posters, and more. In addition to these tasks, I’m also involved in building websites, which has fueled my growing interest in web development.

I specialize in backend development, and I am proficient in tools like Excel, Word, and other office software. I have also honed my skills in automation using Python, which has allowed me to streamline processes and make workflows more efficient.

Throughout my career, I have embraced the opportunity to learn and grow, and I continue to explore new technologies and methodologies to expand my expertise. On this website, you’ll find details about the projects I’m working on, as well as some of my previous work.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with you and exploring future opportunities!
